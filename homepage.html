About:
ZeroProject is a save-directory/chapter select online program for the indie game Night In The Woods. If you have lost your save, chances are that we probably have it here. There are 160+ save files spanning over two timelines, which have been standardized, and their qualities ensured. ZeroProject is currently in its infancy and will be expanded as time continues to go on. It's version is currently 0.9b. It is maintained and kept by IncogniStructura @IncogniStructura#5102 on Discord., incognistructura@gmail.com on email.

The project was originally created when I, like so many others, encountered a technical error in Night In The Woods and was unable to continue my savegame. Furious with not having saved backups, I played on both timelines, collecting over 160 different neccessary saves, and pledging that'd I'd release a software to deal with this by 1 May, and to help those still struggling in a pool of technical errors, asking people on the sub-reddit if they had a familar save. Well, no more. Simply press "Launch", pan to your save, click on it, and press download. You're four clicks away from your save.
